bruh summon -- Summons bot to voice channel
bruh Play URL -- Plays URL of a Youtube Video 
bruh Play TITLE -- Searches Youtube for Video with specified Title 
bruh Skip -- Skips song
bruh remove X -- Removes X song from queue
bruh LQ/LoopQueue -- Loops the current queue
bruh q/queue -- Displays queue
bruh dc/Disconnect -- Disconnects bot
bruh stop -- Stops song
bruh resume -- Starts song 
bruh pause -- Pauses song
