Music Commands :musical_note: 
Play URL -- Plays audio from youtube URL
Play TITLE -- Plays audio from youtube video with title
Skip -- Skips the current audio
Resume -- Resumes playing
Pause -- Pauses the audio
Queue -- Displays the current queue
Remove X -- Removes video at index X in queue
Loopqueue/Lq -- Loops the current queue
Dc/Disconnect -- Disconnects bot from the channel

Economy Commands :moneybag: 
Baltop -- Displays top balances in server
Balance/Bal -- Displays your balance
Hourly -- Can be used hourly to give pocket money
Pay M @ -- Gives money M to user mentioned
Charity M -- Gives money M to Farrah

Casino Commands :8ball:
Coinflip M G -- Coinflips, where M is the bet and G is the guess (heads/tails)
Coinflip M G @ -- Coinflips as before, but forcibly makes @User enter opposing side
Blackjack M -- Starts a game of Blackjack, where M is the bet
